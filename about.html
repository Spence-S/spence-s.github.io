WHO IS SPENCER SNYDER?
Much less mysterious than the title the catchy title would lead one to believe, I am just a pretty normal guy. And, as a normal guy, I like most normal guy things. Things like my lovely girlfriend, my amazing genius sweetest-dog-in-the-world dog, Duchess, working out, drinking a cold beer on the weekends, and finding the fun in the little things in life.
However, there is a much darker side to me. A side of me that likes to sit and contemplate a multitude complicated minutae into the early hours of the morning. A side that keeps me from getting sleep. A side that makes me anti-social, and even stranger, happy to be that way. This dark side, is really not so dark at all. In fact, it's almost certainly the side of me you are most interested in reading about, and the reason there is even anything here to read. All of this side of me stems from one thing. I. f***ing. love. to. write. code.
